About us
medVerse is an all-in-one medical website that allows you to log and keep track of Your health information so that you can easily share and discuss that information with your doctor(s) during your next visit, keeping track of this information on a paper is a time-consuming, manual process. We are here to help you find and book an appointment right from the comfort of your homes, avoiding the long waiting times in the hospitals.
